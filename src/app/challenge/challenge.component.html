What is the challenge?

Our challenge is to identify local sources of carbon emissions and/or estimate amounts of carbon emissions for different human activities to aid scientists in mapping carbon sources and sinks.


Look in to the future

Our team expected that this solution can make people change their perspective, thinking and ignorance about carbon footprint and its consequences. Also, we expected that authorities make changes or enact laws that act against this problem.

It is important to start taking care of our planet, not only for us but for our decendents, with this idea we expect that the world can change in good shape.

What is our solution?

Our solution is a Web page that allows users to know what their carbon footprint is as well as how much our planet needs to do to reduce it.

With this idea it is expected that people can raise awareness about the climate change and its causes.

Idea's Description

  • Users can calculate their carbon footprint inserting some values about their daily activities.
  • The idea will show and communicate the carbon footprint of emissions sources to local authorities through social networks, so they can take actions about it.
  • Information an datasets to build the idea were taken of space agencies and local repositories.
  • Web Frameworks technologies were used to create a better visualization of data and make a friendly interaction with users.
